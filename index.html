This is a non-profit and not any official league, but to coordinate among ourselves, play some healthy cricket (in and around the Dallas, TX area), to avoid checking every weekend with other teams, and to make our weekend practice games more meaningful, interesting, and competitive, we are conducting this. No one will be held responsible for any concerns or issues, individuals/teams involved should resolve on their own (by seeking support if required), Ground reservation is by themselves, and volunteers will be supporting as much as they can.
